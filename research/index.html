Research Interests

I am focused on designing state estimation and control algorithms for rigid objects under various constraints to achieve specific control objectives. My work prioritizes engineering applications. In aerospace, I develop navigation and control algorithms for spacecraft under practical limitations (e.g., loss of inertial information, gyroscope failures, actuator saturation). For UAVs, I incorporate diverse measurements (including visual data and sensors) with dynamics to design algorithms. I also pay attention to visual navigation algorithms under deterministic system framework, such as SLAM observers. Additionally, utilizing various control techniques, I am committed to obtaining algorithms with different stability propertiesï¼Œsuch as global exponential stability and global finite-time stability. Finally, I am also interested in the state estimation methods based on stochastic system framework, such as the family of Kalman filters.

My research points include the following:

  • Spacecraft dynamics, navigation and control
  • State estimation and control for rigid body
  • Visual navigation
  • Theoretical techniques towards stronger stability
  • Estimation filters
