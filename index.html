About Me

I am now a doctoral student at Beihang University, advised by Prof. Haichao Gui, focusing on spacecraft intelligent navigation and control technology. I expect to earn my Ph.D. in June 2026. Previously, I received my B.S. degree in Aerospace Engineering at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics.

The key words for my research are navigation, control, estimation, spacecraft, and UAVs. I am focused on designing state estimation and control algorithms for spacecraft and UAVs under different limitations for various missions, based on rigorous control theories. In addition, I am committed to developing algorithms with various stability properties, utilizing different theoretical techniques for control. Finally, I am also interested in the estimation algorithms under stochastic system framework, such as Kalman filters.

I hope my research can contribute something different to the field. In daily life, I strive to be a resilient person. During my spare time, I enjoy traveling on a budget, cycling, and table tennis.
