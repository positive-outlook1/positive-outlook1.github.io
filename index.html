About Me

I am a M.S. Student at Beihang University, focusing on robotics and artificial intelligence. Before that, I received my B.S. degree majoring in Robotics Engineering at Harbin Institute of Technology.

My research mainly focuses on Robot Learning - to enable machines to intelligently interact with the world, improve themselves over time and acquire novel skills via machine learning (supervised Learning, reinforcement learning, imitation learning, etc.). More precisely, enhancing decision-making in the perception, planning and control phases for robotic agents (collaborative robots, quadruped robots, humanoid robots, aerial robots, mobile robots, etc.).
