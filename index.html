About Me

I am now a doctoral student at Beihang University, focusing on spacecraft intelligent navigation and control technology. Before that, I received my B.S. degree majoring in Aerospace Engineering at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics.

The key words for my research are navigation, control, and spacecraft. I am focused on designing state estimation and control algorithms for spacecraft under different limitations for various missions, based on rigorous control theories. I am also interested in the algorithms under stochastic system framework, such as Kalman filters.My research mainly focuses on Robot Learning - to enable machines to intelligently interact with the world, improve themselves over time and acquire novel skills via machine learning (supervised Learning, reinforcement learning, imitation learning, etc.). More precisely, enhancing decision-making in the perception, planning and control phases for robotic agents (collaborative robots, quadruped robots, humanoid robots, aerial robots, mobile robots, etc.).
