About Me

I am a M.S. Student in the School of Astronautics at Beihang University, advised by Prof. Rui Zhong, Prof. Yinghong Jia, and Prof. Haichao Gui. Before that, I received my B.S. degree majoring in Robotics Engineering at Harbin Institute of Technology.

My research mainly focuses on Robot Learning - to enable machines to intelligently interact with the world, improve themselves over time and acquire novel skills via machine learning.
